Our Vision

Fragment9 aims to redefine cybersecurity through intelligent, machine learning–driven tools that proactively defend creators, users, and digital systems against modern threats.

Our vision is to make advanced threat detection and data protection accessible and privacy-focused—fusing research with real-world application. As we continue building, Fragment9 remains in its development phase, focused on prototyping, testing, and validating open-source tools that bridge innovation and defense.

What We Do

Fragment9 is an early-stage cybersecurity startup and R&D lab designing machine learning-based security tools across multiple domains: threat detection and anomaly-based IDS, phishing detection using NLP, data leak and privacy breach monitoring, and malware analysis with behavioral classification.

We're currently in stealth mode, focusing on open-source tooling, lightweight deployments, and real-world usability as we lay the groundwork for potential SaaS offerings and contributions to the broader cybersecurity community.

Who We're For

We support independent creators, digital entrepreneurs, and small organizations who want to stay secure without big budgets. Our tools are designed for those who need enterprise-level protection with accessible, privacy-focused solutions.
